# Component Inventory - AYANA Bali

This document logs the core UI components identified on the AYANA Bali landing page.

## 1. Navigation Header
*   **Structure:** Floating/sticky translucent or solid header depending on scroll position.
*   **Content:**
    *   Left side: Brand logo SVG ("AYANA").
    *   Center (Desktop): Navigation links (Hotels, Dining, Spa, Experiences, Weddings, Meetings, Offers, Resort Map, Curated Journeys, Access).
    *   Right side: Language select dropdown + "Book Now / Reserve" CTA button.
*   **Interactions:** Hamburger menu overlay toggle on mobile view.

## 2. Hero Slider / First View (`.bl_fv`)
*   **Structure:** Fullscreen background media player (looping background video or fallback high-resolution background WebP).
*   **Content:**
    *   H1 overlay: "AYANA BALI".
    *   Central control button: Play/Mute toggle.
    *   Sticky map/resort areas sidebar (links to: Resort, Segara, Spa, Villas, Garden, Rimba, Kubu Beach).
*   **Interactions:** Hover effects on resort area items, auto-playing video with smooth transitions.

## 3. Four Hotels Section (`#hotels`)
*   **Structure:** Grid layout showing the four primary properties.
*   **Items:**
    1.  AYANA Villas Bali
    2.  AYANA Segara Bali
    3.  AYANA Resort Bali
    4.  RIMBA by AYANA Bali
*   **Content:** Card layout with large representative photo, description, and link CTA.

## 4. Pools & Beaches Section (`#pools`)
*   **Structure:** Horizontal scroll or grid showing pools.
*   **Items:** Ocean Beach Pool, Segara Pool, RIMBA Cabana Pool, Kubu Beach, AYANA Kids Pool.

## 5. Dining Section (`#dining`)
*   **Structure:** Showcase grid of 30 restaurants & bars.
*   **Featured Items:** Rock Bar, Kubu Beach Club, Sami Sami, DAVA Steak & Seafood.

## 6. Spa Section (`#spa`)
*   **Structure:** Showcase highlighting spa treatments.
*   **Items:** Thalassotherapy Pool, Balinese Massage, AYANA Spa, Spa on the Rocks.

## 7. Experiences Section (`#experience`)
*   **Structure:** Grid/Carousel of resort activities.
*   **Items:** Family activities, Sunrise Yoga, Tennis, Kampoeng Bali, Sunset Romantic Dinner, AYANA Farm.

## 8. Footer (`.c_footer`)
*   **Structure:** Large multi-column sitemap.
*   **Content:**
    *   Property directory links (BALI, KOMODO, JAKARTA, CRUISE).
    *   Sitemap pages lists.
    *   Social icons (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, TripAdvisor).
    *   Language switch selector.
    *   Newsletter subscription input.
    *   Copyright & Privacy links.
